Who is this guy?

I'm a programmer and researcher working out of Vancouver, BC. My primary research interests are vision science, human-computer interaction, usability, and data science. I also love to learn about computers in general and social psychology. My hobbies are painting, cooking, music, swimming, and cycling.

Work

I'm currently a laboratory manager & project lead at UBC's Visual Cognition Lab. Previously, I've been a software developer for Tapestry and a data programmer at SportStats.
